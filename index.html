Our Mission
It is our purpose to provide safe, affordable and high quality early childcare to a diverse
multinational community, and to ensure the needs of children and families in the Douglas County

Our Values & Vision
TinyToes Promotes inclusion, courage, creativity, integrity and mutual respect for all.
Our goal is to ensure that our children supported and nurtured to develop self-learners.
We believe that, our approach to education produces independent and confident children,
who are emotionally balanced.
