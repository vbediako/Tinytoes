TinyToes Montessori


Tinytoes Montessori a non profit organization seeking to provide afforable with high qualty
individualized learning experience.Our approach to learning is instilled in each child from
3 months to 8 years and to provide services on occasions. Tinytoes was established
in 2022 with it's main branch serving Western Metropolitan parts of Atlanta, Ga.


Montessori involves child led activities to develop independence learning. It is
amongst one of the fastest growing method to teaching. Our method of teaching
is child specific centered, and we beleive each child is unique. We use
different modes of teaching to acheieve academic excellence. We ensure that
the social, emotional, intellectual and physical development of each child is met.


We also utilize cognitive and cultrural based programs for children ages 3 months to 8 years.
Children are encouraged, and to learn at their own pace and from each other; we encourage
respect and tolerance in our Montessori environment to create academic excellence.
